US Taido Karate Association at the University of Georgia

Taido Karate is a 21st century martial art that originated in Okinawa Japan. In 1975, Mitsunobu Uchida opened the first Taido Karate dojo in the United States which has grown ever since. Taido Karate at UGA aims to spread the art of Taidoka and the characteristics that it imbeds into its students at the University of Georgia through weekly practices that are both physically intensive and allow for mental growth.
