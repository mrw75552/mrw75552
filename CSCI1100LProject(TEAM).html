US Taido Karate Association at the University of Georgia

The team:

Masato Webb - 19 - Second Degree Black Belt

Masato is currently a freshman here at UGA and is an intended-Management Information Systems major who is also pursuing a Sustainability Certificate. He has practiced Taido since the age of 8 and has participated in multiple national and international competitions. Not only has Masato participated in Taido in the US, but he has also travlled to Japan to practice Taido with his fellow students abroad.

Rohan Lall - 24 - Second Degree Black Belt

Rohan is a first year graduate student who is pursuing his Masters in Biochemistry. He also attend UGA during his undergraduate years and has continued to practice Taido ever since leaving Atlanta. Rohan has numerous international tournament appearances under his belt and has also placed many times.

Jaren Mendel - 21 - Second Degree Black Belt

Jaren is a third year undergraduate student at UGA who is majoring in Accounting. Jaren has been ranked in international tournaments numerous times and has a global reputation for being one of the most competitive and high level Taido practioner. He is also the founder and leader of Karma Coffee which is non-profit local organization that has promoted a more positive community here in Athens.
